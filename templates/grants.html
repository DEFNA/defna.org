About the Grant

{% if cycle and cycle.description %}
{{ cycle.description|linebreaksbr }}
{% else %}

DEFNA's community grants program supports members of the Django community who want to organize their own Django-related events across North America. Whether it's a local meetup, a study group, or a mini-conference, we want to help you make it happen.

Grants of up to $300 are available to cover costs such as venue rental, catering, materials, or promotion. Applications are reviewed by the DEFNA board, and a board member will reach out after you submit.
