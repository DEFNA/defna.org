Where your donation goes

DEFNA is a volunteer-run nonprofit. Donations go directly to programs that support the Django community.

Diversity Tickets

We fund reduced-price and free tickets to DjangoCon US for attendees from underrepresented groups, making the conference accessible to more people.

Financial Aid

Travel and accommodation grants help speakers and attendees who couldn't otherwise afford to participate in DjangoCon US.

Community Event Grants

Grants of up to $300 help community members organize their own Django events across North America, growing the ecosystem locally.

{# ── FAQ ── #}

Frequently Asked Questions

Is DEFNA a registered nonprofit?
Yes. DEFNA is a California-based nonprofit organization, established in 2015, and recognized by the IRS as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit.
Are donations tax-deductible?
Yes. As a recognized 501(c)(3) organization, donations to DEFNA are generally tax-deductible for U.S. taxpayers. Please consult a tax professional regarding your specific situation, and contact us if you need documentation of your donation.
Can I donate from outside the US?
Yes — PayPal accepts international credit and debit cards.
Can I donate anonymously?
Yes. PayPal allows you to complete a donation without us publicly identifying you.
Can my company sponsor DEFNA?
Absolutely. Corporate sponsorships are a great way to support the Django community. Get in touch to discuss sponsorship options.
